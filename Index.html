Getting some sushi.

While the page might be interesting, the real pleasure is in enjoying sushi.

The first way to obtain the heavenly bites is to find us on the market,

We have two options available, take away in a box or a paper plate to eat on the market.
When eating on the market you simple need to ask for a small or big paper plate,
we generally recommend a large plate when taking more than 8 pieces of sushi.

For take away there are plastic plates for you to select your sushi which we will box for you.
